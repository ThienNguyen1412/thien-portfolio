Hospital Patient Dashboard

Power BI Data Modeling EHR Analysis DAX

Context & Problem

Modern healthcare facilities generate massive amounts of Electronic Health Records (EHR). Doctors and administrators often struggle to get a quick, comprehensive overview of an individual patient's medical journey. The objective of this project was to build a robust data model and design a centralized Patient Dashboard that consolidates clinical and financial data.

01. Data Architecture & ERD

Before building visuals, a solid foundation was required. I designed a relational data model (Star Schema approach) to connect fragmented hospital systems.

  • Fact & Dimension Tables: Centralized `patient_info` acts as the core, linking to critical operational tables like `appointment`, `patient_tests`, and `Bills`.
  • Inventory & Pharmacy Tracking: Connected `medical_stock_info` with `medicine_patient` to accurately track drug administration and stock levels.
  • Time Intelligence: Integrated a `CALENDAR` table to enable precise time-based DAX calculations (e.g., length of stay, monthly revenue).
Focus: 1-to-Many Relationships, Data Normalization.

02. Patient Insights & Financials

The final Power BI dashboard provides an immersive, holistic profile for each patient, aiding clinical decisions.

  • Holistic Profile: Instantly displays demographics, primary diagnosis (e.g., Acid Reflux), and calculates the exact admission duration.
  • Financial Transparency: Breaks down the total bill (e.g., 43.11K) into specific categories like Surgery, Room, and Tests, ensuring clear billing.
  • Medication Adherence: Uses a custom calendar matrix to track medicine intake by day, helping doctors monitor treatment effectively.
Visuals: Matrix Calendar, Bar Charts, Custom UI.
