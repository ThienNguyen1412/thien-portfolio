Recruitment Portal Admin Dashboard

Dashboard UI Data Tracking System Management Web Analytics

The Context & Goal

Managing a growing recruitment platform ("Thiện Ý") requires a centralized command center. The goal of this project was to design and develop an Admin Dashboard that provides real-time tracking of system health, user growth (both candidates and employers), and interactive charts to analyze job posting workflows and application trends.

01. System Overview & KPIs

The main hub provides administrators with a bird's-eye view of platform activity and operational tasks.

  • Real-time Metrics: Live tracking of essential KPIs including 710 Employers, 412 Candidates, and 627 Active Jobs.
  • Operational Workflow: A dedicated "Pending Approvals" module allows admins to quickly moderate new postings from companies, ensuring quality control.
  • System Health: Integrated monitors to check database connectivity and server stability at a glance.
Focus: UI/UX Layout, Data Aggregation, Admin Workflow.

02. Job Moderation Status

Ensuring the quality and status of job postings is a key administrative task.

  • Workflow Management: This pie chart provides a clear breakdown of job posting statuses (Tỷ lệ Trạng thái Tin đăng).
  • Status Tracking: Admins can easily monitor the proportion of jobs that are Approved (Đã duyệt), Pending (Chờ duyệt), or Rejected (Từ chối) for any given month, helping to prevent moderation bottlenecks.
Visuals: Moderation Workflow Tracking.

03. Engagement & Activity Trends

Interactive data visualization is used to track the correlation between employer demand and candidate supply.

  • Time-Series Analysis: The line chart tracks "New Jobs" (Tin mới) vs. "Applications" (Nộp đơn) on a daily basis for November 2025.
  • Peak Detection: The visual clearly highlights a significant spike in user activity between the 18th and 22nd of the month, with application rates heavily mirroring the influx of new job postings.
Visuals: Dynamic Line Charts, Date-range Filtering.

04. Market Demand Distribution

Understanding which sectors attract the most talent is crucial for targeted marketing and platform growth.

  • Sector Breakdown: The dashboard includes an application ratio analysis grouped by industry (Tỷ lệ ứng tuyển theo ngành).
  • Dominant Sectors: The visualization reveals that Information Technology (CNTT) and High-Tech Agriculture are among the leading sectors, providing actionable insights for the sales team to target specific employer profiles.
Visuals: Responsive Pie Charts, Categorical Data Breakdown.
