Sales & Customer Dashboard 2024-2025

Power BI DAX Star Schema Business Intelligence

The Challenge & Goal

An electronics retail company needed a comprehensive view of their 2024 and 2025 performance. The goal was to move beyond simple spreadsheets and create an interactive tool to evaluate Year-over-Year (YoY) growth, diagnose profitability issues at the product level, and identify VIP customer segments for targeted marketing.

01. Sales Performance Insights

The Sales view provides a high-level overview of revenue vs. profit dynamics.

  • The Growth Paradox: Revenue hit a record $19.5M (+17.7% YoY), yet total profit dropped by 12.9%. This immediately flags an urgency to investigate rising operational costs or COGS.
  • Product Profitability: While Laptops drive revenue, accessories like Cooling Pads and Servers are operating at a loss (negative margin), suggesting a need for pricing strategy revision.
Key DAX Used: YoY Growth %, Moving Average 3M, Margin %.

02. Customer Behavior Analysis

Shifting focus to the 1,139 active customers to understand retention and value.

  • Shopping Patterns: The distribution histogram reveals that most customers fall into the "mid-range" order frequency (600-855 units), indicating a stable customer base.
  • VIP Identification: We successfully isolated the top 10 most profitable clients. The top customer alone contributed over $554K profit. These insights are crucial for designing exclusive loyalty programs.
Data Model: Star Schema connecting Sales fact table with Customer dimension.
